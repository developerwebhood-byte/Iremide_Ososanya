About Me

"I design intuitive digital experiences that solve real problems and improve how people interact with technology."

Hi, I'm Iremide Ososanya, a UX/UI designer who sees design as more than just how something looks, but how it makes people feel. I've always been curious about the small moments most people overlook, like the frustration of not finding something quickly or the relief when an app works exactly the way you expect it to. Those moments are what pulled me into UX and continue to shape how I think as a designer.

I began my UX/UI journey two years ago during my first year of college, where I was introduced to user-centered design. Since then, I've developed a strong interest in user research, wireframing, and prototyping. I enjoy breaking down problems, understanding user behavior, and turning ideas into thoughtful, interactive experiences. For me, the most rewarding part of design is knowing that even a small decision can completely change how someone experiences a product.
